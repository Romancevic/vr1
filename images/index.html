About
MAXY Construction

Maxy Construction Ltd has been established in 2015. Our company has built up an enviable reputation for its work within the industry which includes 3 years restoration works in Temparate House in Royal Botanic Gardens, UNESCO world heritage site.

Our company specialize in installation of balconies, staircases, balustrades, handrails, fences, gates, platforms, walkways, cladding panels, structural frame and temporary steelwork. Our installation team operates to highest standards in the work place.

Maxy Construction Ltd mission is to ensure our client receive outstanding quality products. We are looking forward for new challenges and projects.
